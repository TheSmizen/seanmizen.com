What is NotA Festival?

It's Not A Festival. Stuck between reality and a hypothetical reality, NotA Festival is a music and philosophy spectacular which runs every year on the 29th of February (not on Leap Years). It's been posited that NFest is a bit useless, arbitrarily defined, or even non-existent, but proponents would put their their tongues out and make a prrpt noise. Nobody's really sure whether it actually happens, but check out this whizz-bang website and decide for yourself.
